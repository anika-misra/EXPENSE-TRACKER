# EXPENSE-TRACKER
An expense tracker is a useful tool designed to help individuals record, monitor and manage their daily expenditure in an organized way. Its main purpose is to give users a clear understanding of how they are spending their money. This helps in people's financial planning and decision making. 
# OBJECTIVE
The main objective of an expense tracker is to help individuals manage their finances effectively by keeping a clear and organized record of their daily expenses. It aims to provide users with a better understanding of their spending habits so they can make informed financial decisions.
Another key objective is to promote financial discipline by encouraging users to track every expense, no matter how small. This helps in identifying unnecessary or excessive spending and allows individuals to control their budget more efficiently. The expense tracker also aims to simplify financial planning by providing summaries of daily, weekly, or monthly expenses.
Additionally, it helps users set spending limits and achieve savings goals by monitoring where their money is being used. For students and individuals with limited income, it serves as a practical tool to avoid overspending and manage resources wisely.
Overall, the objective of an expense tracker is to improve financial awareness, support budgeting, and develop responsible money management habits.
# FEATURES
An expense tracker includes several useful features that make managing finances simple and effective:
1. Add and Record Expenses:
Users can easily enter daily expenses with details like date, amount, and description.

2. Categorization of Expenses:
Expenses can be divided into categories such as food, travel, shopping, bills, and entertainment for better organization.

3. Expense Summary:
Provides a clear overview of total spending on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

4. Budget Setting:
Users can set spending limits for different categories to control their expenses.

5. Visual Reports:
Displays data using charts or graphs, making it easier to understand spending patterns.

6. Search and Filter Options:
Allows users to find specific transactions quickly based on date or category.

7. Edit and Delete Entries:
Users can update or remove incorrect expense records when needed.

8. User-Friendly Interface:
Designed to be simple and easy to use, even for beginners.

9. Data Storage and Security:
Keeps financial data stored safely for future reference.

10. Notifications or Reminders:
Reminds users to track expenses regularly or alerts them when they exceed their budget.
