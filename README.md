# EXPENSE-TRACKER
An expense tracker is a useful tool designed to help individuals record, monitor and manage their daily expenditure in an organized way. It's main purpose is to give users a clear understanding of how they are spending their money. This helps in people's financial planning and decision making. 
# OBJECTIVE
The main objective of an expense tracker is to help individuals manage their finances effectively by keeping a clear and organized record of their daily expenses. It aims to provide users with a better understanding of their spending habits so they can analyse the money spent.

Another key objective is to promote financial discipline by encouraging users to track every expense, no matter how small. This helps in identifying unnecessary or excessive spending and allows individuals to control their budget more efficiently.

Overall, the objective of an expense tracker is to improve financial awareness, support budgeting, and develop responsible money management habits.

# FEATURES
An expense tracker includes several useful features that make managing finances simple and effective:
1. Add and Record Expenses:
Users can easily enter daily expenses with their amount.

2. Categorization of Expenses:
Expenses can be divided into categories such as food, travel, shopping, bills, and entertainment for better organization.

3. Expense Summary:
Provides a clear overview of total expenditure.

 4. Delete Entries:
Users can remove incorrect expense records when needed.

5. User-Friendly Interface:
Designed simple and easy to use, even for beginners.

6. Data Storage and Security:
Keeps financial data stored safely for future reference.
