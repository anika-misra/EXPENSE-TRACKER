# PROBLEM STATEMENT 

Many individuals, especially students, struggle to keep track of their expenses on everyday basis, which leads to poor financial management and overspending. 
An expense tracker is needed to help keep record of expenses, manage budgets, and make better financial decisions.

# SCOPE OF VARIABLE
1. Amount- Shows the value of the expense entered by the user.
2. Category- Identifies the type of expense (food, travel, etc.).
3. Total expense- Calculates the sum of all expenses

 # LIMITATIONS
 1. Cannot accept negative or invalid values.
 2. Limited to predefined categories.
 3. Depends on correct input data, no errors are to be made.

# TARGETED USERS
1. Students- They often have limited budgets and need to monitor daily expenses like food, travel,study materials, etc.
  
2. Young Professionals- Who want to track their salary, bills to save and budget.

4. Freelancers/ Part-time Workers - Who manage irregular income and need to keep records of earnings and expenditures.
   
5.  Households- Individuals or families looking to monitor monthly spending and regulate unnecessary costs.

6. Anyone interested in financial planning – Users who want to gain insights into spending patterns, set budgets, and achieve savings goals.

# HIGH LEVEL FEATURES
1. Add and Manage Expenses – Users can record daily expenses to keep a check on expenditures.

2. Categorization of Spending – Expenses are organized into categories such as food, travel, bills, and entertainment for easy tracking.

3. Delete Records – Existing entries can be removed for accuracy and better organization.
